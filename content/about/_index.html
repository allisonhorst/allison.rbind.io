Hi, I’m Allison! I’m a continuing lecturer at the Bren School of Environmental Science and Management (UC Santa Barbara) where I teach statistics, data analysis and science communication.

I’m also an artist with a background in plein air landscape oil painting. Recently I’ve moved into digital art and design, and am currently an Artist-in-Residence at the RStudio.

I split my time between mountains in Santa Barbara and Mono counties, where I enjoy hiking, fly-fishing, and wandering aimlessly in oak forests and sagebrush with my partner Greg and our dog Teddy.
