Hi, I’m Allison! I’m a lecturer at the Bren School of Environmental Science and Management (UC Santa Barbara) where I teach statistics, data analysis and presentation skills.

I’m also an artist with a background in plein air landscape oil painting. Recently I’ve moved into digital art and design, and am currently an Artist-in-Residence at the National Center for Ecological Synthesis and Analysis.

I split my time between Santa Barbara and the Eastern Sierra Nevada, and enjoy hiking, fly-fishing, and wandering aimlessly in the sagebrush with my partner Greg and our dog Teddy.
